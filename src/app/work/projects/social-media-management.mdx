## Case Study: 8% Engagement Boost + 15K Followers

**Client**: Fashion & Lifestyle Brand  
**Platforms**: Instagram, TikTok, Facebook  
**Duration**: 6 months

**Challenge**:
- Inconsistent posting schedule
- Low engagement rates (0.8%)
- Stagnant follower growth
- No clear content strategy

**Strategy**:
- Developed comprehensive content calendar
- Created mix of educational, entertaining, and promotional content
- Implemented UGC strategy with 5 creators
- Daily engagement and community building
- Trend-jacking on TikTok and Instagram Reels
- Strategic hashtag research and implementation

**Results**:
- **8% increase** in engagement rate (0.8% → 8.8%)
- **15K+ new followers** across platforms
- **1.4M+ organic reach** in 6 months
- **100% impression growth** month-over-month
- **350+ SKUs sold** through social commerce
- **5 viral posts** with 100K+ views each
